Engineered for Research Excellence

Seamlessly bridging the gap between scientific literature, omics data, and AI-driven analysis.

Hardware-Aware RAG

Features a dynamic allocation algorithm that intelligently scales embedding (e.g., BGE-M3) and reranker parameters based on your available system RAM and GPU VRAM.

OA PDF Retrieval Cascade

Built-in MCP server executes a robust fallback strategy (Semantic Scholar → Unpaywall → PubMed OA) to securely fetch full-text Open Access PDFs globally.

Multi-Omics Data Mining

Directly interfaces with NCBI tools to query SRA datasets, GEO expression studies, and retrieve raw FASTA sequences for rapid biological validation.

Modular Architecture

Highly extensible foundation utilizing BaseTool classes. Researchers can rapidly develop and integrate custom analysis scripts as independent plugins.

System Requirements

Scholar Navis automatically scales its AI models to match your hardware. Choose the tier that best describes your workstation.

Portable / Minimal

CPU-Only execution (Nano & Lite Models)

  • RAM: 2 GB Minimum
  • GPU: Not Required
  • Disk Space: ~2 GB

Utilizes ultra-fast parameters suitable for pure CPU environments and 2-in-1 portable devices.

Recommended

Scientific Standard

GPU Recommended (M3 Series Models)

  • RAM: 8 GB Minimum
  • VRAM: 6 GB (e.g., RTX 3060/4060)
  • Disk Space: 5 GB for Models

Flagship scientific models. Excellent understanding of academic terminology with multi-language and long text support.

Extreme Workstation

High-End GPU Only (Gemma 9B / NV-Embed)

  • RAM: 32 GB - 64 GB
  • VRAM: 20 GB - 24 GB+
  • Disk Space: 20 GB - 35 GB

Unlocks state-of-the-art semantic understanding and massive parameter precision for local NLP tasks.
